*The Faithful Servant* explores the character and responsibility of those entrusted with God’s work. It calls readers beyond activity into a life marked by obedience, stewardship, endurance, and accountability before the Master.

The planned resource family includes the edited book, devotionals, reflection questions, prayers, Scripture highlights, a multimedia teaching series, and printable study resources.
