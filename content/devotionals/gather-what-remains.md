After Jesus fed the multitude, He instructed the disciples to gather the fragments. The miracle had already happened, yet stewardship was still required.

Some of the most valuable lessons in our lives remain in fragments: a sentence from a sermon, a prayer answered slowly, a failure that revealed our weakness, or an insight we wrote down but never revisited.

## Pay attention to what remains

Not every season ends with a polished conclusion. Sometimes what remains is enough for the next assignment. Gather the lesson. Preserve the testimony. Write down the insight. Share what can nourish someone else.

Faithfulness includes recognizing value where others see leftovers.
