Genesis presents a pattern: the Spirit moves, God speaks, and light appears. This pattern helps us understand spiritual transformation.

The Holy Spirit prepares the heart, brings conviction, and illuminates truth. The Word reveals Christ, creates faith, corrects error, and gives form to a new way of life.

## Study questions

- What happens when people pursue the Word without dependence on the Spirit?
- What happens when people claim spiritual experience without submission to Scripture?
- How can your personal study become more prayerful and responsive?
