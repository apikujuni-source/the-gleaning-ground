Spiritual maturity is not measured primarily by vocabulary, visibility, gifting, or years in church. Scripture points us toward Christlike character and faithful responsibility.

## Five marks of maturity

1. **Increasing responsiveness to God.** Mature believers do not merely hear; they obey.
2. **Stability under pressure.** Trials reveal whether convictions have become character.
3. **Discernment.** Maturity learns to distinguish good from evil and truth from attractive error.
4. **Responsibility for others.** Mature sons become nourishment, encouragement, and guidance to others.
5. **Christlike love.** Knowledge and gifting are governed by love.

## Group discussion

Which of these marks is strongest in your life? Which requires intentional growth? What practical habit would support that growth?
