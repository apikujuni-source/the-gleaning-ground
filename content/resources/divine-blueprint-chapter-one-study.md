## Study objective

Identify the condition of humanity apart from Christ and understand how God begins the work of spiritual recreation through the Holy Spirit, His Word, and the Light of men.

## Key Scriptures

Genesis 1:1–3; John 1:1–12; John 16:7–8.

## Discussion

1. What do “without form,” “void,” and “darkness” reveal about life apart from God?
2. Why does God address darkness first?
3. How does the Holy Spirit prepare a person for transformation?
4. What act of obedience will help you walk in the light this week?
