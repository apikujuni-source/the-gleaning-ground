Use these questions at the end of a month, study series, or significant season.

- What truth has become clearer?
- Where has God exposed immaturity?
- Which spiritual habit became more consistent?
- What act of obedience did I delay?
- How have I served or strengthened someone else?
- What is God inviting me to practice next?
